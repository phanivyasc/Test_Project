About

Prithvi Farms trusts earth In it's natural form and thus avoids any kind of artificial elements that would harm nature. If you are looking for a fresh, healthy and naturally grown vegetables at your door step directly from the farm, we are here for you.

Goal

Organic farming is giving back the nature to itself. Basically farming is just to use the nature's resource for human needs but on the go it became a demand due to less supply. The green revolution is the main cause to affect the nature and soon it will impact us. Natural farming is the best way to leave back the nature to itself.
